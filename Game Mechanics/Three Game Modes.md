## Three Game Modes

### Ship/Space Mode

When the crew is operating as a unit aboard their ship or in space-based scenarios.

- **Shared Character Sheet**: All crew abilities are combined
- **Additional Skills**: Piloting, Navigation, Sensors, Ship Systems
- **Example**: "The crew has Combat 5, Technical 7, Piloting 3"

### Crew Mode

When the crew is working together on-planet or in stations, but not using the ship.

- **Shared Character Sheet**: All crew abilities combined (same as Ship Mode)
- **Excluded Skills**: Ship-specific abilities (Piloting, Navigation, etc.)
- **Example**: "The crew has Social 6, Investigation 4, Medical 2"

### Tactical Mode

Individual combat when diplomacy fails and the crew must act separately.

- **Individual Character Sheets**: Each crew member has their own stats
- **Additional Stats**: Health/Stamina, Movement, Accuracy, Initiative
- **Example**: "Dr. Chen has Combat 1, Health 8, Movement 2"
