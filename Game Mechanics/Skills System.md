
## The 8 Core Skills

- **Combat:** Fighting, weapons, tactical combat
- **Technical:** Engineering, repairs, hacking, systems
- **Medical:** Healing, biology, xenobiology, diagnosis
- **Social:** Communication, negotiation, leadership, deception
- **Investigation:** Analysis, research, problem-solving, forensics
- **Stealth:** Infiltration, hiding, quiet movement, security
- **Science:** Physics, chemistry, xenobiology, theoretical knowledge
- **Piloting:** Ship flying, navigation, vehicle operation

## System Notes for Events

**Difficulty Guidelines (2d6 + Skill):**

- **Easy (5+):** Routine professional work - Masters succeed 94%, Experts 83%
- **Medium (7+):** Standard challenges - Masters succeed 83%, Experts 72%
- **Hard (9+):** Significant challenges - Masters succeed 72%, Experts 58%
- **Very Hard (11+):** Elite challenges - Masters succeed 42%, Experts 28%
- **Near-Impossible (13+):** Legendary - Masters succeed 17%, Experts 8%

**Special Results:**

- Natural 2 (snake eyes): Always failure (critical failure if appropriate)
- Natural 12 (boxcars): Always critical success
- Beat difficulty by 4+: Critical success
- Miss difficulty by 4+: Critical failure

**Using This System in Events:**

- Identify required skills for each action
- Set appropriate difficulty
- Masters are reliable (72% on hard tasks)
- Experts are competent (58% on hard tasks)
- Untrained characters struggle (28% on hard tasks)
- Specialist selection matters significantly
